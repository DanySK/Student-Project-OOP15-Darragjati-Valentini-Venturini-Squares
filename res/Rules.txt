Game's rules

Standard mode:
At the start of the game, is asked to the players to set the dimensions of the playable field.
After that, they can set one move per turn, between two point not already linked. 
When one player sets the fourth side of a square, he earns a point and gains an extra move. 
When there are no more moves left, the games ends with a winner.

Triangle mode:
If the players choose the triangle mode, they should make a triangle instead of a square to score a point.
